## FOR THE FARMER

### Section A: Background (5 minutes)

1. How many years have you been farming rice in New Bataan?
2. How many hectares do you currently farm?
3. Have you experienced rice disease outbreaks before? Which diseases?
4. How do you normally find out if your rice has a disease?

### Section B: System Demonstration (10 minutes)

*[Ask the farmer to use the app. Observe and take notes.]*

5. Please take a photo of this rice leaf using the app. What happened?
6. Can you read the result? What does it say?
7. Is the fertilizer recommendation clear to you? Would you follow it?
8. Please try the Chat button. Ask it a question about rice farming.

### Section C: Usability (10 minutes)

9. Was the app easy to use for the first time? What was confusing?
10. Did the chatbot answer your question correctly?
11. Would you use this app every week to check your rice?
12. Would you trust the app's disease detection over your own judgment?
13. What would make you use this app more often?

### Section D: Impact (5 minutes)

14. Do you think this app can help reduce crop losses in New Bataan?
15. Would you share this app with other farmers in your barangay?
16. Is there anything the app cannot do that you wish it could?

---

## FOR THE AGRICULTURAL TECHNICIAN

### Section A: Background (5 minutes)

1. How long have you been working as an agricultural technician in New Bataan?
2. What are the most common rice diseases you see in New Bataan?
3. How do farmers currently report disease problems to your office?

### Section B: Technical Review (15 minutes)

4. I will show you the app's disease detection results. Are they technically accurate?
5. Review the fertilizer recommendations — are they consistent with DA guidelines?
6. Review the AI chatbot answers — are they scientifically correct?
7. Did you find any incorrect information in the chatbot?

### Section C: Professional Opinion (10 minutes)

8. Can this system supplement or complement DA extension services?
9. What are the risks if the AI makes an incorrect diagnosis?
10. How would you recommend farmers use this tool alongside DA assistance?
11. Is the DA contact information included in the app appropriate?
12. Would you recommend the DA office officially endorse this system?

### Section D: Improvements (5 minutes)

13. What additional agricultural knowledge should the chatbot include?
14. Are there features specific to New Bataan rice farming that should be added?
15. Would you be willing to participate in a future system improvement workshop?
